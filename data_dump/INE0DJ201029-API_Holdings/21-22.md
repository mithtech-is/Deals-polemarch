| P&L | Revenue **POSITIVE**, expenses **POSITIVE** | `changes_in_inventory` and `deferred_tax_expense` keep natural sign (negative if in brackets) |
| Cash Flow | Inflows **POSITIVE**, outflows **NEGATIVE** | Brackets in PDF = negative |
| SCE | Opening equity **POSITIVE** | Losses are negative |
